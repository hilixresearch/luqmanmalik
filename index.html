I am currently accepting mathematics, computer, and engineering students. I offer support with Core Standards for K-12ers in Bay Area school districts. I also cover undergraduate math, physics, and EE/CS. Stop by Alameda Math Group and Alameda Math Circle to find program information. We welcome all Alameda-based Putnam enthusiasts and Olympians. For all interested in computers, I will be volunteering Wednesdays and Saturdays at TechExchange, who are currently accepting Chromebooks or laptops with Intel i5, above, or equivalent, and MacBook 2010s or later that can be refurbished for student use. For in-person instruction (given Covid-19), ideal settings are private yards or cafes.

Our location offers ample space for social distancing. We apply disposable coverings to each work surface, and keep a full store of sanitizing solutions and facial masks. Most virtual sessions will be held via Zoom.
