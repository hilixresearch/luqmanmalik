The Moiré Effect

In geometry, Moiré patterns are large-scale interference patterns that are generated when opague-ruled patterns with transparent gaps are inlaid with similar patterns. For this moiré interferencing to occur, the two patterns must not be completely identical, but rather e.g. displaced, rotated, or posess slightly different pitch. Below, we will create a circular, rotating moiré-disc, using purely HTML/CSS.
